Yuting Hong

Email  /  GitHub  /  Google Scholar

Yuting is a CS Ph.D. Student at South China University of Technology, advised by Dr. Xiaowei Hu. Prior to that, he obtained Master degree at Ningbo University advised by Dr. Chengbin Peng, and Bachelor degree at Shaoxing University.

He used to work as a machine learning engineer at Om AI Lab, advised by Dr. Ruochen Xu.

His research interests include embodied AI, vision-language models and agent systems. He focuses on building AI systems that connect perception, language, reasoning, and action. He used to focus on computer vision in data-scarce scenarios, including semi-supervised learning, domain adaptation, and long-tail learning.
